## üí≥ How to Subscribe & Pay

### Payment Methods Available

We accept all major Philippine payment methods:

| Method | How to Pay | Processing Time |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| **GCash** | Enter GCash number, confirm in app | Instant |
| **Maya** | Enter Maya details, confirm | Instant |
| **Credit/Debit Card** | Enter card details | Instant |
| **QR Ph** | Scan QR with any bank app (RCBC, BPI, UnionBank, etc.) | Instant |

### Step-by-Step Payment

1. Go to **"Pricing"** or tap **"Upgrade"**
2. Select your plan (Pro or Enterprise)
3. Tap **"Subscribe"**
4. Choose payment method:

#### Paying with GCash:
1. Select **"GCash"**
2. Enter your GCash-registered mobile number
3. Tap **"Pay"**
4. Check your GCash app for confirmation
5. Enter MPIN to confirm

#### Paying with Maya:
1. Select **"Maya"**
2. Enter your Maya account details
3. Tap **"Pay"**
4. Confirm in Maya app

#### Paying with Card:
1. Select **"Credit/Debit Card"**
2. Enter card number
3. Enter expiry date (MM/YY)
4. Enter CVV (3 digits at back)
5. Enter cardholder name
6. Tap **"Pay"**

#### Paying with QR Ph (Any Bank):
1. Select **"QR Ph"**
2. A QR code will appear
3. Open your bank app (RCBC, BPI, UnionBank, etc.)
4. Go to "Scan QR" or "QR Payments"
5. Scan the code
6. Confirm payment amount
7. Enter your bank app PIN/password

### After Payment

- **Success**: You'll see confirmation and your plan upgrades immediately
- **Receipt**: Sent to your email
- **Invoice**: Available in your account settings

## üìä Understanding Your Estimate

### Cost Breakdown Sections

#### 1. Structural Works (Foundation & Frame)
- **Concrete**: Foundation, slab, columns
- **Rebar**: Steel reinforcement
- **CHB**: Concrete hollow blocks for walls
- **Cement, Sand, Gravel**: Basic materials

#### 2. Architectural Works (Finishes)
- **Roofing**: Longspan metal roofing
- **Ceiling**: Gypsum boards
- **Flooring**: Tiles
- **Painting**: Interior and exterior
- **Doors & Windows**: Panel doors, sliding windows

#### 3. Electrical Works
- **Wiring**: 14/2 and 12/2 gauge wires
- **Outlets & Switches**: Standard outlets
- **Lighting**: LED downlights
- **Panel Board**: Circuit breakers

#### 4. Plumbing Works
- **PVC Pipes**: 1/2", 3/4", 1" pipes
- **Fixtures**: Water closet, lavatory, shower
- **Water Heater**: (if applicable)

#### 5. Miscellaneous
- **Scaffolding**: Rental cost
- **Site Cleanup**: Post-construction cleaning
- **Temporary Facilities**: Site office, storage

### Quality Tiers Explained

| Tier | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Basic** | Entry-level materials | Budget housing, rentals |
| **Standard** | Good quality, common brands | Typical residential |
| **Above Standard** | Premium brands, better finish | Mid-high end homes |
| **Premium** | Luxury materials, imported | High-end, luxury homes |

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In
- Check email and password
- Try "Forgot Password"
- Ensure internet connection
- Clear browser cache and retry

### Payment Failed
- Check balance in GCash/Maya/Card
- Try different payment method
- Contact your bank if card declined
- Try again after 15 minutes

### App Not Loading
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Restart your phone

### Prices Seem Wrong
- Prices update every 3 days
- Check "Last Updated" date
- Tap "Update Prices" in Settings
- Prices vary by region

### Estimate Seems Too High/Low
- Check quality tier selected
- Verify floor area is correct
- Compare with local market rates
- Contact support for review
